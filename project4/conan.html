Conan Exiles

Conan Exiles is a multiplayer world that takes place in a remote desert, secluded from the rest of the world. Battle your way as a barbarian and conquer these ruthless lands! As your quest unfolds, you'll be presented with the challenges of survival, not only from the environment, but from those who inhabit it! Can you tame the strongest beasts and conquer those who seek to stand in your way? Challenge yourself like ever before!
