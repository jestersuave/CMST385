Supreme Gaming

About us:

Supreme Gaming is a family of stable and long-standing game servers. Our goal is to provide survival servers that reduce unnecessary parts of the grind and incease dynamic gameplay and enjoyment. As such, several of our servers offer a more convenient approach to setup. As players know, the unofficial scene is plagued by the quick rise and death of servers that disappear without a trace, admins that exhibit blatant corruption, pay-to-win incentives, frequent wipes, and lack of player communities. Supreme Gaming seeks to alleviate that and offer an accelerated and unique experience through boost rates and Quality of Life (QoL) modes and features.
