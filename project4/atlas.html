Atlas

Atlas is a multiplayer world that takes place on the high seas. Become the best pirate you can be, claim islands, collect bounties and treaure! As your quest unfolds, you'll be presented with the challenges of survival, not only from the environment, but from those who inhabit it! Can you tame the strongest beasts and conquer those who seek to stand in your way? Challenge yourself like ever before!
